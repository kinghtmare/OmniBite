# OmniBite  

This tool helps you create a personalized nutrition and workout plan based on your goals. Follow these steps:  

## How It Works  

### 1. Calculate Your Calorie Intake  
   - Use the [TDEE Calculator](https://tdeecalculator.net/) to determine your Total Daily Energy Expenditure. *(Page 1)*  

### 2. Get Your Body Metrics  
   - The tool provides your:  
     - Ideal weight  
     - Body Mass Index (BMI)  
     - Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)  
   *(Page 2)*  

### 3. Select Your Plan  
   - **Goal**: Choose between *Maintain*, *Bulking*, or *Cutting*.  
   - **Carb Preference**: Pick *Low Carb*, *Moderate Carb*, or *High Carb*.  
   *(Page 3)*  

### 4. Receive Your Custom Plan  
   - Get a **2-day plan** tailored to your needs:  
     - **Rest Day Plan**  
     - **Training Day Plan**  
   - Includes:  
     - Meal splits (calories per meal)  
     - Number of meals per day  

### 5. AI-Powered Ingredient & Resource Search  
   - The tool uses AI to:  
     - Search YouTube and the internet for relevant recipes/tips.  
     - Identify labeled ingredients from trusted brands.
